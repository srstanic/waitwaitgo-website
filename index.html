WaitWaitGo!

Simple Rest Timer App for iOS

What can you do with it?

  • it’s a stopwatch
  • start the countdown by pressing one of the time buttons
  • see the remaining and passed time
  • reset it if you don’t need it anymore
  • change the preset times

What is it used for?

It's primarily used for timing rest periods during a strength training workout. Let your phone notify you when it's time to start the next set or exercise.

Change the time interval while the timer is already running

The app recalculates the remaining time and keeps the time that already passed.

Sometimes there is just not enough energy in the tank and you need an extra minute to rest.

Respects your phone battery, your music and your ears

Saves battery. Works in the background and with the screen locked. Sound warning lets you know when it's time to get ready for the next set.

Uses carefully selected custom sounds that play nice with your workout music. Noticeable enough not to miss it, but still easy on your ears.

No need to look at the phone or a clock at all.
Just focus on visualising your next set.

Actionable iOS notification

iOS notification appears when the time is up. You are notified with a sound to start the next set.

When you’re done with the set, you can even restart the timer without unlocking the screen! No need to think about how long is your rest time after each set.
