WaitWaitGo!

Simple Rest Timer App for iOS

What can you do with it?

  • it’s a stopwatch
  • start the countdown by pressing one of the time buttons
  • see the remaining and passed time
  • reset it if you don’t need it anymore
  • change the preset times

What is it used for?

It's primarily used for timing rest periods during a strength training workout. Let your phone remind you when it's time to start the next set or exercise.

What makes this one better than the others?

I'm glad you asked. WaitWaitGo! is the best rest timer app out there. I know because I tried them all. No kidding, I installed and used 12 different timer apps on my device. Only after none of them worked as I wanted them to, I decided to make my own.
